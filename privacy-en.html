RankIt — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

This privacy policy explains how the RankIt mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and protects user data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

The App does not request or collect personal identity information such as your name, email address, or phone number. To operate and improve the App, the following technical data is processed:

  • Anonymous identifier (Firebase Anonymous Auth): A random, anonymous user ID is assigned to your device so that multiplayer games can function. This ID cannot be linked to your real identity.
  • Usage data (Firebase Analytics): Events such as which screens are opened, game starts/wins, and general usage statistics are collected anonymously.
  • Crash logs (Firebase Crashlytics): If the App crashes or errors, technical crash reports (device model, operating system version, stack trace) are collected so we can fix the issue.
  • Advertising identifiers (Google AdMob): The advertising identifier (Advertising ID) and related ad data are used to display rewarded ads.

Additionally, information such as your score and the questions you have solved is stored only locally on your device and is not sent to us.

2. How We Use the Data

  • To match and synchronize multiplayer games,
  • To measure app performance and improve the experience,
  • To detect and fix crashes and errors,
  • To display ads that support free gameplay.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics) — Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (ads) — Google Privacy Policy

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data, please contact us at the address below and we will delete the relevant data.
